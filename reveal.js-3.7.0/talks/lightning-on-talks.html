But what about my slides?

Its easy to write slides that are walls of text. But remember that as soon as you put something in front of your audience they'll start reading it. And in general, people are very bad at focusing on two things at once. So guess what, you just lost your audience and now need to pull them back into your talk.

  1. 95% of you just lost the thread of this talk
  2. But that's okay, that was the point.
